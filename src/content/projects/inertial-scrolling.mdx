Implemented an experimental demo of alternative inertial scrolling techniques, under the supervision of Prof. Viktor Kaptelinin, based on his inertial scrolling method **[US Patent 12,399,610 B2](https://patents.google.com/patent/US12399610B2/en)**.

# Scrolling techniques

### Highlighted Break-Contact
Highlights the document area touched at the break-contact point during inertial scrolling.

Demo: **[Highlighted Break-Contact](https://arashg.dev/scrolllab/?type=II&content=TEXT)**

### Limited Distance (LD)
The inertial scrolling distance is constrained by the **break-contact point** (where the user’s finger leaves the touchscreen).  
Scrolling is limited to the distance between the break-contact point and the **window border** in the scrolling direction.

Demo: **[Limited Distance](https://arashg.dev/scrolllab/?type=III&content=TEXT)**


### Limited Distance + Highlight (LDH)
Same as **LD**, but additionally highlights the document area touched at the break-contact point during inertial scrolling.

Demo: **[Limited + Highlighted](https://arashg.dev/scrolllab/?type=IV&content=TEXT)**
